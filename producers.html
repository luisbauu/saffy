BANDULF INDUSTRIES

Bandulf Industries is a producer of wooden products made out of acacia wood in San Carlos, Pangasinan. Their products include kitchen utensils, plates, bowl, trays, chopping boards, and wall decors.

The father of Randy De Vera started this business and it was passed on to him. He is now running the business alongside his wife, Venus de Vera. The loving couple have raised their 3 children and have provided livelihood to their workers through their woodcraft business.

They attend local trade fairs to market their products and they supply for export companies including Saffy, Inc.

ALCADEZA NATIVE PRODUCTS

Alcadeza Native Products is a family business of the Herreras in Daraga, Albay which was legally registered in the year 2018.

Elvie Herrera is currently heading the business and is helped by her family and relatives whenever there are ongoing orders. Their raw material is Abaca, of which their province is known for, and their products include Christmas decors, ornaments, etc.

The Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) gave them opportunities to attend local trade fairs as an exhibitor and thus they were able to meet local clients and organizations that may aid them including Saffy, Inc.

JOGERMZ HANDICRAFTS TRADING

Jogermz Handicrafts is a group of Filipino artisans producing Capiz handicrafts in Caloocan City, Philippines. It used to be called with DTCL Handicrafts way back 2013, wherein all of the artisans were previous in-house Capiz workers of Saffy.

They also employ women, unemployed and self-supporting college students in the neighborhood whenever they receive volume orders. They are able to provide livelihood in the community and they plan to continuously train people in the community who are interested in the handicrafts.
